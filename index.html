FAQ

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

What types of cargo do you handle? +
We handle a wide range of cargo, including general freight, oversized equipment, perishable goods, hazardous materials, and time-sensitive deliveries. Contact us for custom solutions tailored to your shipment.
Do you offer international shipping? +
Yes, we offer reliable international shipping to destinations worldwide. Whether it's air, sea, or land freight, we ensure secure and timely delivery with full documentation support.
How do I get a quote? +
Fill out the quote form on this page or call us directly. Provide shipment details — origin, destination, weight, and dimensions — and we'll respond with a tailored quote within 24 hours.
Can I track my shipment in real time? +
Absolutely. We provide live shipment tracking through our online portal. You'll receive a tracking number once your shipment is dispatched so you can monitor status at every stage.
Do you handle customs clearance? +
Yes, comprehensive customs clearance is part of our service. Our experienced brokers handle all documentation and compliance requirements for smooth, hassle-free import and export.
Are shipments insured? +
All shipments can be fully insured. We offer standard cargo insurance and enhanced coverage options for high-value goods. Ask your account manager for details.
